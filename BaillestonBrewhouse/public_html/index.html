What We Do

We use local ingredients from the Glasgow area to brew our finest lagers, stouts and ales. We take influence from the local people and environment when making our beer. Having the local Campsie hills so close to the Clyde ship yard meant that we could use these influences to make some delicate fruity beers but also make some strong, dark stouts and ales. We aim to be a brewery of the people and try to show this in our craft.
